WHAT IS MECCATRONICA ?

Meccatronica club is an independent club that works the fulfillment of the technical knowledge of the engineering students to be familiarized with the new trends in engineering and give them practical exposure in the field of mechatronics.

Meccatronica is Italian name of Mechatronics.
Mechatronics = Mechanics + electronics + software

  • Learn new skills in the mechanical or software field.
  • Use Mentorship Program.
  • Interaction with Industrial and Academic experts in the Domain of Club Theme.
  • Quiz and Group Discussions.
  • Conduct Workshops and Practical Training Sessions.
  • Work on Industrial Projects.
